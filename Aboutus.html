Photography is my favorite hobby. It refreshes the mind and provides a welcome change from such dull and dreary routine of daily life. I am particularly interested in capturing landscapes, especially during twilight....Photography teaches me a lot of useful things. I have to take all aspects of things into consideration. I have to look from all directions to capture a picture. Color combination is also a very important factor. While taking pictures of animals or birds I have to wait for hours and I learned about patience.
Photos provide a more emotional attachment than words often do. They often capture the atmosphere that I cannot find the words for.
Photographs, perhaps more than anything to me, I believe that they are the documentations of our lives. The ability of photography to connect to our past, associate us in an event, and preserve memories without words or interpretation makes it an influential hobby for all of us.
